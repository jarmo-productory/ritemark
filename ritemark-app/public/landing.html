Frequently Asked Questions

What is RiteMark?
+

Visual markdown editor for non-technical users. Format text with toolbar buttons, export clean markdown for AI tools and GitHub. No syntax memorization—start writing in 30 seconds.

Is my data secure?
+

Yes. Documents save to YOUR Google Drive—we can't read them. Only files you create or open with RiteMark are accessible. Revoke access anytime from Google Account settings.

Is RiteMark free?
+

Yes. Free and open-source. Documents use your Google Drive storage (15GB free, or upgrade to Google One). No hidden fees, no premium tiers, no credit card.

Do I need to know markdown?
+

No. Edit visually with toolbar buttons—markdown conversion happens automatically. Only see markdown when you export to AI tools or GitHub.

What makes RiteMark unique?
+

Visual editing only: Format with buttons, export markdown. Never see syntax while writing—perfect for non-technical users.

Your Drive, your files: Documents saved to YOUR Google Drive. We can't read them. Revoke access anytime from Google settings.

AI-optimized output: Copy clean markdown directly to ChatGPT, Claude, or GitHub. No format conversion, no cleanup.

Security & Privacy First

Your data stays yours. Complete transparency on how we protect your privacy.

Google OAuth2 Authentication

No passwords to manage. Sign in securely with your Google account using industry-standard OAuth2 protocol.

  • No password storage on our servers
  • Secure token-based authentication
  • Automatic session expiration

Zero Data Collection

We don't collect, store, or analyze your documents. Your content exists only in your Google Drive.

  • No server-side document storage
  • No analytics or tracking scripts
  • No third-party data sharing

Minimal Google Drive Permissions

We only request access to files you create or open with RiteMark. We cannot see your other Drive files.

  • Per-file access (drive.file scope)
  • No full Drive access requested
  • Revoke access anytime from Google

100% Open Source

Full source code transparency. Anyone can audit our security practices and verify our privacy claims.

  • Inspect all source code on GitHub
  • Community security audits
  • Self-hosting option available

GDPR-Ready by Design

Built with European privacy standards in mind. Your data sovereignty is guaranteed.

  • Data stays in your Google Drive
  • Right to deletion (revoke OAuth)
  • No cross-border data transfers

Only Google Drive API

We use exclusively Google's official Drive API. No third-party cloud services or data processors.

  • TLS encryption in transit
