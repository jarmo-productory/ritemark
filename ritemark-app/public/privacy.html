Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 5, 2025

RiteMark ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our WYSIWYG markdown editor.

Information We Collect

When you sign in to RiteMark using Google OAuth, we access:

  • Email address - To identify your account
  • Name - To personalize your experience
  • Profile picture - To display in the user interface
  • Google Drive access - To save and sync your documents (limited to files created by RiteMark)
Important: We only request access to Google Drive files that you create or open with RiteMark. We cannot see or access your other Google Drive files.

How We Use Your Data

Your information is used exclusively for:

  • Authenticating your identity
  • Displaying your name and profile picture in the application
  • Saving and retrieving your markdown documents from Google Drive
  • Enabling real-time collaboration features (future)

Data Storage and Security

RiteMark is a client-side application that runs in your browser. Your data is handled as follows:

  • Local Storage: Authentication tokens are stored in your browser's sessionStorage (cleared when you close the browser)
  • No Server Storage: We do not store your Google credentials, documents, or personal information on our servers
  • Google Drive: Your documents are stored in your own Google Drive account, encrypted by Google
  • HTTPS: All communication is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS

Third-Party Services

RiteMark integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google OAuth & Drive API: For authentication and document storage (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Netlify: For hosting the application (Netlify Privacy Policy)

Cookies and Tracking

RiteMark does not use cookies for tracking or analytics. We use sessionStorage only for maintaining your authenticated session while you use the application.

Data Retention

  • Session Data: Cleared when you close your browser or sign out
  • Documents: Remain in your Google Drive until you delete them
  • No Analytics: We do not collect or retain usage analytics

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data at any time through your Google Account
  • Delete your documents from Google Drive
  • Revoke RiteMark's access to your Google account at any time via Google Account Permissions
  • Request data deletion (though we don't store personal data on our servers)

Children's Privacy

RiteMark is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

Open Source

RiteMark is an open-source project. You can review our code and security practices on GitHub.
